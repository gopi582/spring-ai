ABC Technologies Employee Policy

Working Hours:
Employees are expected to work from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Monday through Friday.

Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to:
- 12 Casual Leaves per year
- 12 Sick Leaves per year
- 10 Public Holidays

Remote Work:
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Code of Conduct:
Employees must maintain professionalism, respect colleagues, and protect company information.

Performance Review:
Performance reviews are conducted twice every year.

Training:
Employees are encouraged to participate in technical and soft-skill training programs.
